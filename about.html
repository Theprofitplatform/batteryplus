Our Story

Founded in 2010 by automotive expert Nilesh Rohan, Batteries Plus NSW began as a small workshop in Parramatta with a simple mission: to provide NSW with the most reliable battery services available.

What started as Nilesh's one-man operation serving local car owners has grown into NSW's most trusted battery specialist, with a team of certified technicians serving thousands of customers across the state. Our founding principle remains unchanged: every customer deserves power they can depend on.

Under Nilesh's leadership, we've expanded our services to include commercial fleets, emergency vehicles, marine applications, and specialized battery solutions. Today, we're proud to be the go-to battery experts for individuals, businesses, and government agencies throughout New South Wales.
