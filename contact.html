Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

We provide services across all of Sydney metropolitan area and throughout NSW. Our mobile service covers a 50km radius from our main workshop, with extended coverage for emergency services.

Our emergency response time is typically 30-60 minutes within Sydney metropolitan area, and up to 2 hours for regional NSW locations. We maintain a fleet of mobile service vehicles strategically positioned across the state.

Yes, we can provide indicative quotes over the phone for standard battery replacements. However, for accurate pricing on complex systems or installations, we recommend an on-site assessment which is free of charge.

We accept cash, EFTPOS, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), bank transfers, and offer account terms for approved commercial customers. All major payment methods are available for both in-store and mobile service visits.

Absolutely! All our batteries come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 1-5 years depending on the type. Our installation and service work is backed by a 12-month workmanship warranty. Emergency callout services include a 30-day guarantee.

Yes, we offer comprehensive battery testing services using professional-grade equipment. Testing can be done at our workshop or on-site with our mobile service. We provide detailed reports on battery condition, capacity, and expected lifespan.
